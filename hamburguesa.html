Hamburger

Description

The Hamburguer is a Sandwich with meet between two breads.

Is a food from Germany or United States and his fame came with fast food.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meet or Chicken
  • Bacon
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Tomatoe
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Take the meat out of the fridge an hour before preparing it.
  2. Heat the skillet or griddle.
  3. Sprinkle or brush the burger with olive oil and put it in the pan.
  4. Make sure the heat is not too high to prevent it from overcooking on the outside and remaining raw on the inside.
  5. Cook it as you like: medium, rare, well done...
  6. Never mash the meat with a spatula or poke it with a fork while it is cooking, as it loses its juices and makes it much less flavorful.
  7. Give it just one spin.
  8. Have the garnish ready: chopped vegetables, cheeses, sauces, among others.
  9. Open and toast the bread of your choice.
  10. Place the burger on the bun, incorporate the ingredients and add your favorite sauces.

That's all!
